Inclusive

Design for all potential users. Attorneys come from very diverse backgrounds. We should take into consideration limitations, advantages, etc when designing UIs.

Consistent

Constistency is critical to good usibility. It doesn't mean everything has to be a complete replication. Using the same elements in various ways escapes the mundane and creates uniformity ensuring a stable user experience. Consistency also helps user confidence in knowing they've executed correctly and after repeated use, seeing the same elements creates familiarity that the users begin to trust.

Curious

Engage users with curiosity by designing delightful experiences that encourage creative problem solving and critical thinking. Practice good UI, give users the right tools at the right moments. Keep it simple and focus on the content.

Human

Always design with the user in mind. Embrace empathy. We're not just designing a good product, but helping people simplify their work life. We design and build from understanding our customer through extensive user reasearch, prototyping, and usibility testing. We're humans helping humans.
