Colors of Best Case

Best Case Desktop has always focused on the content and functionality first and foremost sticking to a mostly gray palette. The new Best Case Online builds from the roots of the legacy software. This is achieved by sticking with the classic light gray workspace background. We introduce deep vibrant colors only when needed that serve a worthwhile purpose.

Note: Color scales provide more options to the designer. Unless specified, use the base scale.
