The Best Case Logo

The Best Case logo consists of two elements, the Sphere and the Wordmark. For all intended purposes the logo should be used as displayed below. The two elements should never be resized, distorted, animated, or used independently of one another unless approved.

Logo Minimum Size

Logo size is important. The Best Case logo should never be reduced smaller than 150px width. Certain instances may arise for smaller or larger uses and will be addressed accordingly.

Logo Clear Space

In order for the logo to breathe and gain attention, no external elements should come within one pedal-width from the logo on any side. The size of the pedal space is relative to the size of a pedal on the spere for the logo being used.

Logo Variations

Different backgrounds and environments have different requirements. We provide a few options to best represent the brand logos in certain settings. Download the logo you need from M:\Marketing\Branding\Logos. Not sure wich logo to use, please email brand@cingroup.com for assistance.

Best practices

  • Use font sizing, and weight to create hierarchy and focus.
  • Identify proper page hierarchy and carry it through
  • Evaluate all the visual elements (size, padding, spacing) before going down a rabbit hole.
  • Accommodate for visual deficiencies such as low vision and color blindness.
  • Use proper contrast to achieve the legibility of the design.
  • Avoid eye strain (such as incompatible color combinations).
  • Use color with purpose. Color alone should not drive a reaction/decision.
  • Understand color associations across cultures and their meanings.
